Duration and easing tokens for micro-interactions. 150–300ms keeps UI responsive. Durations collapse to 0ms when prefers-reduced-motion: reduce is active.

r-card-action

Small overlay button used in the top-right corner of image cards. Translucent white background with rounded corners. The --star modifier applies gold color for favourite actions.

.r-card-action .r-card-action--star
Tokens used: 26×26px · radius 6px · bg rgba(255,255,255,0.9) · star color #F59E0B

r-tooltip

Dark popover that appears on hover/focus. Uses a ? trigger icon by default. Positioned above the trigger with a caret arrow. High z-index (1000) to ensure it renders above cards and overlays.

Card: 2.9% + $0.30 ? This is the calculation used for the donor tip. Updating here will also update other campaigns that use this Stripe account. Processing fee ? Adding this small percentage will help cover platform costs.
.r-tooltip-wrap > .r-tooltip-trigger + .r-tooltip — hover or focus to reveal
Tokens used: bg #1a1a2e · text #fff · radius --r-radius-md · shadow --r-shadow-md · z-index 1000

Button States

Interactive states for buttons: disabled (opacity + cursor change), loading (spinner overlay), focus-visible (keyboard ring), and active (press scale). Tab through the buttons below to see focus rings.

Tab to these buttons to see :focus-visible ring:

.r-btn:disabled | .r-btn--loading | :focus-visible | :active
Tokens used: disabled opacity var(--r-opacity-disabled) · focus ring var(--focus-ring) · active scale 0.97
